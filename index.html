User Research Assignment

Persona # 1

Name: Stephanie Ramia

Demographics: Age: 27, Gender: Female, Location: Westminster - CO

User Needs: Accessing and using the Blackboard website for Westwood College through http://mypath.westwood.edu to access online courses, discussions, online quizzes, lab connections, and projects.

User Goals: To turn in assignments, take online tests, and participate in online discussions with other students in the class and with remote students as well.

Motivation: To pass the course successfully with an A+ grade, and also to fully understand the practical part of the course.

User Frustrations: Loading documents to Mypath is a big frustration because files with a size of 512 MB or greater cannot be uploaded easily. The discussion board is not clear, and also cannot track the grades for the discussions.The user might think that the website needs to be fully changed to something else.
